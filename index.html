Hi we're *James and Amit*. We love to make art and to code, and especially to mix the two together.
Seeing our young kids learn to count made us want to explore the simple act of counting from *zero to ten* in weird and wonky ways. That's how Numby was born.

We hope you enjoy Numby. Today he just counts to Ten and back again, but we'll continue to teach Numby new tricks in small increments, and surprise with new updates.

**Why Numby knows about Zero**
Counting seems like such a simple thing to do, but even we computer coders make silly mistakes, especially because of the number 0. So for his debut, we made sure that Numby also knows about Zero from the get go.

Zero is an important number. Someone even wrote an *entire book about it*!
(http://www.amazon.com/Zero-Biography-Dangerous-Charles-Seife/dp/0140296476)

While most people start counting from 1, in computer programs we typically start counting from 0. Sometimes that gets us coders in trouble, especially when we forget that counting from 0 to 10 takes 11 steps (try counting up to 10 with your fingers but start from 0..1..2..3.. and you'll see!)
